National Taiwan University Girls' Baseball Team




Introduce for NTUGBS

Set up in 1970, our team has 51 years history until now. Although we played softball before, we have turn to plaing baseball in these years. Not afraid of the melted weather and the dirt from the red soil, not afraid of the hardness of the skills and the pain from being hit by balls, the persistence of keep practicing is the only way for us to prove our enthusiasm to baseball.
